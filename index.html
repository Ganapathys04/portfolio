About Me

Who Am I?

I am a dynamic and outgoing individual with a unique blend of technical expertise and a passion for product management, driven by my entrepreneurial spirit. From a young age, as a "daddy’s girl," I was inspired by my father, a businessman, to combine technical prowess with strategic thinking. This led me to pursue a career where I could merge technology with business management.

My academic journey began with a degree in Electronics and Instrumentation Engineering from Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, where I developed a strong technical foundation. Building on that, I earned a master's degree in Management of Technology from Arizona State University (ASU), where I fine-tuned my ability to lead innovative projects in fast-paced environments.

With a background in product management, I’ve honed the skills to tackle complex challenges head-on, bringing teams together to create impactful solutions. My career experiences have taught me the importance of user-centered design, cross-functional leadership, and data-driven decision-making, which I bring to every project I manage.

Outside of work, I’m an adventurer at heart—whether it’s dancing, exploring new tech trends, or challenging myself on the world’s tallest thrill rides, I believe in living life with passion. I also take pride in my determination and resilience; I never leave a task unfinished and push for excellence in every endeavor.My resilience and determination, shaped by my experience as an athlete and volleyball player, inspire me to excel in all endeavors.
